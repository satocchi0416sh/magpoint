# MagPoint Privacy Policy

MagPoint does not collect, store, or transmit any data. Period.

- No analytics, no telemetry, no crash reporting.
- No network requests — the extension runs entirely on your machine.
- No reading or storing of page content. The content script only measures the
  positions of clickable elements on the page you are viewing, in memory, to move
  the highlight. Nothing leaves the tab.
- No accounts, no cookies, no external services.

The single permission MagPoint needs is the ability to run its content script on
the pages you visit (`<all_urls>`), because a pointing aid is only useful if it
works everywhere. That script draws the highlight and routes your click to the
captured element — nothing else.

If this policy ever changes, the change will be visible in this file's history in
the public repository.
